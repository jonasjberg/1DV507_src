Exercise 5
==========
Write a program OneTwoThree.java which creates a JavaFX Application window
containing the following.

1. Three panes with different colors.
2. Each pane should contain a word: One, Two or Three.
3. One should be placed in the upper left corner, Two should be placed in the
   middle of the panel and Three in the lower right corner.
